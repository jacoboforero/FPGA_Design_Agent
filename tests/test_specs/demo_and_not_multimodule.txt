Module: and_not_top
Title: Simple multi-module AND-with-inverted-B demo

Functional intent
Role summary: Top-level integration module that instantiates inv1_cell and and2_cell to compute y = a & ~b.
Key rules:
- inv1_cell computes b_inv = ~b.
- and2_cell computes y = a & b_inv.
- and_not_top performs only structural integration and explicit wiring between child modules.
Performance intent: Same-cycle combinational response from inputs to output.
Reset semantics: No reset; output depends only on current inputs.
Corner cases:
- a=0,b=0
- a=0,b=1
- a=1,b=0
- a=1,b=1
